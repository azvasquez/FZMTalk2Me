Place your Live2D model folder here.

Free moe/bishoujo models to get started:
  • Live2D official samples (Hiyori, Natori, etc.)
      https://www.live2d.com/en/learn/sample/
  • Booth.pm — search "live2d free model"
      https://booth.pm/en/browse/Live2D
  • nizima.com — curated marketplace, has free section
      https://nizima.com

After adding a model, update MODEL_PATH in src/config.js.
Example:
  export const MODEL_PATH = '/models/Hiyori/Hiyori.model3.json';
